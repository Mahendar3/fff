Rohit Sharma is an Indian international cricketer and the captain of Indian cricket team in Test and ODI formats. He is widely regarded as one of the greatest ODI opening batters of all time. He is a right-handed batsman who plays for Mumbai Indians in Indian Premier League and for Mumbai in domestic cricket.
